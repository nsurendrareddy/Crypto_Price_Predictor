About Cryptocurrencies

Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency, widely considered digital gold with a fixed supply of 21M coins.

Marketplace: Binance

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is a programmable blockchain enabling smart contracts and dApps. Transitioned to Proof of Stake in 2022.

Marketplace: Binance

XRP (XRP)

XRP is designed for fast, low-cost cross-border payments, settling transactions in seconds with minimal fees.

Marketplace: Binance

Tether (USDT)

Tether (USDT) is a fiat-pegged stablecoin, widely used for liquidity, remittances, and trading in crypto markets.

Marketplace: Binance

BNB (BNB)

BNB is the native token of Binance Chain, used for trading fees, staking, and powering DeFi apps.

Marketplace: Binance

Solana (SOL)

Solana is a high-performance blockchain for DeFi, NFTs, and games, known for fast and cheap transactions.

Marketplace: Binance

USD Coin (USDC)

USD Coin is a fully-backed stablecoin, regulated and widely used across centralized and DeFi platforms.

Marketplace: Binance

Dogecoin (DOGE)

Dogecoin started as a meme, now a widely used cryptocurrency with low transaction fees and active community.

Marketplace: Binance

TRON (TRX)

TRON is a decentralized platform focused on high throughput, powering dApps and digital content.

Marketplace: Binance

Cardano (ADA)

Cardano emphasizes research-driven development and sustainable blockchain infrastructure.

Marketplace: Binance

Chainlink (LINK)

Chainlink provides secure oracles that connect smart contracts to real-world data.

Marketplace: Binance

Hyperliquid (HYPE)

Hyperliquid is tied to derivative markets for high-frequency decentralized trading.

Marketplace: Binance

Stellar (XLM)

Stellar enables cross-border payments and remittances at very low cost.

Marketplace: Binance

Sui (SUI)

Sui is a high-throughput blockchain based on Move, designed for parallel execution.

Marketplace: Binance

Bitcoin Cash (BCH)

Bitcoin Cash is a fork of Bitcoin optimized for cheaper, faster transactions.

Marketplace: Binance

Hedera (HBAR)

Hedera Hashgraph is a public network offering fast, low-fee transactions with enterprise adoption.

Marketplace: Binance

Ethena USDe (USDe)

Ethena USDe is a synthetic dollar pegged by derivatives to maintain stability.

Marketplace: Binance

Avalanche (AVAX)

Avalanche is a smart contract platform with high throughput and sub-second finality.

Marketplace: Binance

Litecoin (LTC)

Litecoin is a long-standing cryptocurrency, considered the silver to Bitcoin’s gold.

Marketplace: Binance

Toncoin (TON)

Toncoin powers The Open Network (TON) ecosystem for payments and Web3 apps.

Marketplace: Binance
